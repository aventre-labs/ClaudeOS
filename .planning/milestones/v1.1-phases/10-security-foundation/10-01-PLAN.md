<objective>
Secure the setup endpoint against race conditions and migrate the auth model from auth.json/password to CLAUDEOS_AUTH_TOKEN env var.

Purpose: SETUP-04 requires that two simultaneous first-boot requests cannot both succeed. The current BootService has no mutex protection on the setup endpoint. Additionally, the entire password-creation flow is being replaced — auth.json is eliminated, and the encryption key is derived deterministically from the Railway-generated auth token.

Output: Race-condition-protected BootService, SecretStore using scrypt-derived key, passing tests.
</objective>
